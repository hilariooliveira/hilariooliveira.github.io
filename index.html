Hilário Oliveira

Hilário Tomaz Alves de Oliveira is a leading Brazilian Computer Scientist specializing in Artificial Intelligence (AI), particularly Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Text Mining. He holds a Ph.D. (2018) and a Master's (2013) in Computer Science from UFPE, focusing on Automatic Text Summarization and Unsupervised Ontology Population, respectively. He previously worked as a Researcher/Developer at Kurier Tecnologia and on projects with HP Lab (United States) in automatic text summarization.

Currently, he serves as a Professor at IFES-Serra in Control and Automation Engineering and the Postgraduate Program in Applied Computing (PPComp), teaching AI, NLP, and Deep Learning. His research includes Text-To-SQL solutions, chatbot knowledge base optimization, and business process model extraction using Deep Learning. He has registered several computer programs for NLP tasks, including automatic essay analysis for Portuguese. He is also a member of the Brazilian Computer Society (SBC) and the Computational Intelligence and Information Systems Laboratory (LaIcSi).
